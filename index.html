Ankit Burman

Ankit Kumar, known by his stage name Ankit Burman, resides in Gurugram, Haryana, and hails from Olra, Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. He pursued his B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering at WCTM, Gurgaon, showcasing a passion for technology and education in the dynamic landscape of the National Capital Region.
Ankit Burman is a versatile Indian music artist, excelling as a music producer, rapper, lyricist, and expert in music mixing and mastering.
